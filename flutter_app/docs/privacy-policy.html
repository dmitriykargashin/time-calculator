Privacy Policy — Time Calculator Cardamon

Last updated: 19 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the Time Calculator Cardamon mobile app (the “App”, “we”, “us”) handles your information. The App is published by Cardamon Inc. If you have any questions, contact us at support@cardamon.org.

Summary

  • The App is free and shows no ads inside the app.
  • We do not collect personal information, do not require an account, and never sell your data.
  • We collect anonymous usage analytics and your device’s advertising ID — used to improve the App and to measure our Google Ads campaigns.
  • In the EEA, the UK and Switzerland, this is collected only after you consent. You can change it any time in the App under Settings → Privacy.

Information we collect

We use Google Analytics for Firebase to understand how the App is used. Through it, the following may be collected:

  • App activity (app interactions) — which features and screens you use (for example, opening the formats or support screens, or tapping calculator keys).
  • Device or other identifiers — an app-instance identifier that Firebase assigns to your installation of the App, and your device’s Advertising ID, used to measure our Google Ads campaigns and to build advertising audiences. (The App itself displays no ads.)
  • Approximate location — a coarse, city-level location derived from your device’s IP address. We do not collect precise or GPS location.

We do not collect your name, email address, contacts, photos, files, messages, precise location, or any other personal information, and we do not track you across other apps or websites.

Purchases

The App offers optional “buy me a cup of tea” donations through Google Play’s billing system. Payments are processed by Google; we do not receive or store your payment details.

How we use the data

The data is used to understand how the App is used and to improve it (analytics), and to measure and optimise our Google Ads campaigns and build advertising audiences (advertising / marketing). The App itself displays no ads.

Consent and legal basis (EEA / UK / Switzerland)

Where required (the EEA, the UK and Switzerland), we collect analytics only with your consent, which we request when you first open the App. You may withdraw consent at any time in Settings → Privacy. Outside these regions you can also disable analytics in the same place.

Sharing

We do not sell your data. Google Analytics for Firebase processes the analytics data on our behalf as a service provider. For advertising measurement, your Advertising ID and related data are shared with Google (Google Ads) to measure our campaign performance and build audiences. Google’s handling of data is governed by the Google Privacy Policy.

Data retention and security

Analytics data is retained by Google for the period configured in our analytics account and then deleted. All data is encrypted in transit using TLS.

Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to access, correct or delete your data, or to withdraw consent. To withdraw analytics consent, use Settings → Privacy in the App. For any other request, contact support@cardamon.org.

Children

The App is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted at this URL with a new “Last updated” date.
